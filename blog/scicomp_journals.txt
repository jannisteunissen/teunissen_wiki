===== Scientific Computing Journals =====

This mostly serves as a list for myself, but perhaps it can also be useful for
others. Impact factors are given for 2014, if available. Fees for open acces are
also listed.

An excellent list of journals in which you can publish software can be found [[http://www.software.ac.uk/resources/guides/which-journals-should-i-publish-my-software|here]].

==== Mixed access ====

  * [[http://www.journals.elsevier.com/computer-physics-communications/|Computer Physics Communications]] (Elsevier, 3.1)\\ Fee: 2400\$
  * [[http://www.journals.elsevier.com/journal-of-computational-physics/|Journal of Computational Physics]] (Elsevier, 2.4)\\ Fee: 2400\$
  * [[http://epubs.siam.org/journal/sjoce3|Siam Journal on Scientific Computing]] (1.9)\\ Fee: 2500\$
  * [[http://www.global-sci.com/|Communications in Computational Physics]] (1.9)\\ Fee: 1600\$
  * [[http://www.springer.com/mathematics/computational+science+%26+engineering/journal/10915|Journal of Scientific Computing]] (Springer, 1.7)\\ Fee: 3000\$
  * [[http://www.journals.elsevier.com/journal-of-computational-science/|Journal of Computational Science]] (Elsevier, 1.2)\\ Fee: 2400\$

==== Open access ====

  * [[http://www.mdpi.com/journal/computation|Computation]]\\ Fee: 0 -- 250 CHF
  * [[http://openresearchsoftware.metajnl.com|Journal of Open Research Software]]\\ Fee: 100 -- 300 pound
  * [[http://www.journals.elsevier.com/softwarex/|SoftwareX]] (Elsevier)\\ Fee: 500\$
